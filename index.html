Intro

Hi! Welcome to our webpage! We're Tony and Denise and we love camping. Camping, camping, camping. Can't get enough! And while we have been known to go "Glamping" once or twice, our favorite kind of camping is actually backpacking. And our favorite place to backpack camp is Zaleski State Forest. Why? Mostly because it's free, but there's way more we love about it.

According to the Ohio Department of Natural Resources website, Zaleski State Forest is 27,822 acres making it the second largest forest in Ohio's system of state forests. Within the forest is the Selinde Roosenburg Memorial Backpacking Trail of which we have hiked about half.

"The thing I love most about backpacking is the preparation. It really makes you focus. The planning starts, days or even weeks in advance. It's very calming and relaxing and it starts before I've even left!"

— Tony

"Don't get me wrong, I like to Glamp too. But backpacking is peaceful. It's more than quiet. At times it's nearly silent. I'm so tired at the end of a days hike but in the best way."

— Denise
